XMPP Internet-Drafts

Last Updated: 2010-01-06

The base XMPP specifications are RFC 3920 and RFC 3921, which are currently being updated by the XMPP Working Group at the IETF. While most XMPP extensions are defined in the XEP series published by the XMPP Standards Foundation, the following XMPP-related Internet-Drafts are under consideration within the IETF:

  • draft-ietf-xmpp-address-09 (text | HTML) -- a dedicated specification of the XMPP address format, including full Unicode support

  • draft-ietf-xmpp-3920bis-22 (text | HTML) -- proposed revisions to RFC 3920

  • draft-ietf-xmpp-3921bis-19 (text | HTML) -- proposed revisions to RFC 3921

  • draft-ietf-xmpp-dna-00 (text | HTML) -- a proposed method for supporting multiple domains over a single server-to-server connection

  • draft-ietf-xmpp-e2e-requirements-01 (text | HTML) -- requirements and a threat analysis for end-to-end encryption

  • draft-miller-3923bis-02 (text | HTML) -- one possible approach to end-to-end encryption of XMPP traffic, based on X.509 certificates and AES encryption

  • draft-meyer-xmpp-e2e-encryption-02 (text | HTML) -- another possible approach to end-to-end encryption of XMPP traffic, based on Transport Layer Security

  • draft-meyer-xmpp-sasl-cert-management-01 (text | HTML) -- a method for managing multiple end-user certificates related to a particular XMPP account, including best practices for use with SASL EXTERNAL authentication

  • draft-saintandre-atompub-notify-07 (text | HTML) -- transporting Atom syndication data over the XMPP pubsub extension

  • draft-saintandre-sip-xmpp-chat-03 (text | HTML) -- mappings for interoperability between XMPP and the Message Session Relay Protocol for one-to-one chat sessions

  • draft-saintandre-sip-xmpp-core-01 (text | HTML) -- architectural assumptions and address mappings underlying interworking between between XMPP and SIP

  • draft-saintandre-sip-xmpp-groupchat-01 (text | HTML) -- mappings for interoperability between XMPP and the Message Session Relay Protocol for many-to-many chat sessions

  • draft-saintandre-sip-xmpp-im-01 (text | HTML) -- mappings for interworking between between XMPP and SIMPLE IM

  • draft-saintandre-sip-xmpp-media-01 (text | HTML) -- mappings for interworking between between the XMPP Jingle extensions and SIP

  • draft-saintandre-sip-xmpp-presence-02 (text | HTML) -- mappings for interworking between between XMPP and SIMPLE presence

  • draft-saintandre-xmpp-i18n-02 (text | HTML) -- considerations for potential changes to the handling of internationalized addresses in XMPP

  • draft-saintandre-xmpp-presence-analysis-03 (text | HTML) -- an analysis of the scalability of interdomain presence federation using XMPP, for comparision with draft-ietf-simple-interdomain-scaling-analysis

Older versions of these and other drafts are available in the attic (see also the source control repository).
