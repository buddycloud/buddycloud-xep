XMPP Summit

The XMPP Standards Foundation holds a developer conference twice a year, usually co-located with FOSDEM in Europe (winter) and OSCON in North America (summer). Information about the following meetings is available:

  • Summit 9 (Portland, Oregon, July 2010)
  • Summit 8 (Brussels, Belgium, February 2010)
  • Summit 7 (San Jose, California, July 2009)
  • Summit 6 (Brussels, Belgium, February 2009)
  • Summit 5 (Portland, Oregon, July 2008)
  • Summit 4 (Brussels, Belgium, February 2008)
  • Summit 3 (Portland, Oregon, July 2007)
  • Summit 2 (Brussels, Belgium, February 2007)
  • Summit 1 (Portland, Oregon, July 2006)
