XMPP Council

The XMPP Council is the technical steering group that approves XMPP Extension Protocols, as governed by the XSF Bylaws and XEP-0001. The Council is elected by the members of the XMPP Standards Foundation each year in September.

The ninth Council (2009-2010) consists of the following individuals:

  • Dave Cridland (resigned 2010-03-01)
  • Nathan Fritz (joined 2010-03-31)
  • Ralph Meijer
  • Kevin Smith (Chair)
  • Remko Tronçon
  • Matthew Wild
