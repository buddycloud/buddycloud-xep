XMPP ICA: Whois Lookups

When you request a digital certificate from the XMPP ICA as described in the Certificate Issuance HOWTO, our root CA (StartCom) checks various domain information, including a native Whois lookup. Unfortunately, some top-level domains (TLDs) do not offer native (non-WWW) Whois services, so we cannot issue digital certificates for those domains.

This policy affects many TLDs, including (but not limited to):

  • .by (Belarus)
  • .es (Spain)
  • .hr (Croatia)
  • .mk (Macedonia)
  • .za (South Africa)

To see if your TLD offers a Whois service, check the IANA Root Zone Database. If your XMPP server is affected by this policy, please encourage your TLD registrar to offer a Whois service. We apologize for any inconvenience this policy may cause you.
