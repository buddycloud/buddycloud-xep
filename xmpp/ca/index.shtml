XMPP Intermediate Certification Authority

The XMPP Standards Foundation, in cooperation with StartCom, issues free domain certificates to administrators of XMPP-based services. Our goal is to make the XMPP network the most secure open communications network on the Internet. To find out more, visit any of the following pages:

  • Certificate Issuance HOWTO
  • Certificate Installation HOWTO
  • Information for XMPP Developers

The XMPP Intermediate Certification Authority is a service of the XMPP Standards Foundation, as defined in the ICA Proposal approved by the XSF membership on 2006-11-28.
