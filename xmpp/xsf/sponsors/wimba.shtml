XSF Sponsors :: Wimba

Wimba™, a Silver Sponsor of the XMPP Standards Foundation (XSF), is a leading provider of collaborative learning software applications and services to the education industry.

Wimba's collaborative software applications for the online and blended education market enable institutions to bridge technology and pedagogy by supplementing course management systems with many of the proven disciplines of in-person learning environments. Wimba's intuitive solutions enable educators and students to quickly and easily teach and learn live online, engage in live chat and instant message exchanges, benefit from oral content being added to text-based course content, and more.
