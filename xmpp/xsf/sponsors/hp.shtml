XSF Sponsors :: HP

HP, a Gold Sponsor of the XSF through its generous contribution of hardware powering the XSF's computing infrastructure, delivers vital information technology (IT) for business and life. The company's solutions span IT infrastructure, personal computing and access devices, global services and imaging and printing for consumers, enterprises and small and medium business. As a strategic partner with the XMPP Standards Foundation (XSF), HP implements XMPP-based solutions for its customers as well as provides research and development based on Jabber/XMPP technologies, expanding the availability of those technologies to the HP-UX, Microsoft Windows 2000 and Windows 2003 environments, in addition to Linux.
