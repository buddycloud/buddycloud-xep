XSF Sponsors :: ProcessOne

ProcessOne, a Titanium Sponsor of the XMPP Standards Foundation (XSF), specializes in high-performance messaging solutions. The company actively contributes to development of the ejabberd server and offers strong commercial support for ejabberd installations around the world that together are responsible for millions of registered users. ProcessOne also develops high-capacity benchmarking test suites, enterprise instant messaging clients, and software for machine-to-machine communication. The company also provides active support for the ejabberd deployment at the flagship jabber.org XMPP service.

For additional information about the XMPP software and support offered by ProcessOne, visit <www.process-one.net> or contact <mickael.remond@process-one.net>.
