XSF Sponsors :: IBM

IBM is one of the world's leading information technology companies. In 2001, IBM was a founding sponsor of the XMPP Standards Foundation at the Platinum level. IBM has pursued research and development based on Jabber/XMPP (including the SashJab client and work on middleware for its service component architecture), has also implemented Jabber-based solutions for its customers, such as the CapWIN emergency network in the Washington, D.C. area.

For more information about IBM, visit <www.ibm.com>.
