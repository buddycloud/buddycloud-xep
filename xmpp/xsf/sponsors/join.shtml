Sponsoring the XSF

The XMPP Standards Foundation (XSF) is a non-profit, member-run organization whose mission is to develop open extensions to the IETF's Extensible Messaging and Presence Protocol (XMPP), support the Jabber/XMPP community, and promote XMPP for real-time communications. The XSF is able to fulfill that mission in large part because of the generous support of sponsoring companies who are using or building Jabber/XMPP technologies.

For further information about sponsoring the XSF, please view our Sponsorship Program Definition or contact XSF Executive Director Peter Saint-Andre via IM or email at <stpeter@jabber.org>.
