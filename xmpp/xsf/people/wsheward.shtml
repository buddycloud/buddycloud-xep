XSF People :: Will Sheward

Will Sheward is a marketing professional who has spent all of his career in the software industry. He currently works as VP Marketing for Isode Ltd. Will lives just outside London, is a Fellow of the Royal Society of Arts, and has an MBA from Lancaster University's Management School. He is a member of the XSF Board of Directors for 2008-2009 and is determined to help increase the profile and membership of the XSF.
