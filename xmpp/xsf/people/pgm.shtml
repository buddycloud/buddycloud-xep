XSF People :: Peter Millard

Peter Millard was one of the original client developers on the Jabber team, having created WinJab as well as the more recent Exodus client for Windows. Peter was considered an expert on the Jabber protocol; he authored or co-authored a number of XMPP Extension Protocols, and he was a member of the XMPP Council from 2001 to 2005. Peter died on April 26, 2006.
