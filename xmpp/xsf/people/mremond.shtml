XSF People :: Mickaël Rémond

Mickaël Rémond is a software developer and enthusiastic technical evangelist who lives in Paris, France. Mickaël founded XSF Sponsor Process-one after having worked both in startups and large consulting companies. He is now actively involved in promoting XMPP-based messaging solutions and participating to the definition of the roadmap for the ejabberd platform. Mickaël is a member of the XSF Board of Directors for 2008-2009, 2007-2008, and 2006-2007.
