XSF People :: Joe Hildebrand

Joe Hildebrand was a member of the 2003-2003 XMPP Council. He has many years of experience with messaging systems, which he applies enthusiastically to the Jabber world. In addition to creating Jabber-NET and helping out with Exodus, Joe is Chief Technology Officer at Jabber, Inc. and has co-authored a number of XMPP Extension Protocols.
