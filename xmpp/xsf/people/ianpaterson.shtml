XSF People :: Ian Paterson

Ian Paterson has been developing Jabber/XMPP applications full-time since 2001, mainly the Chatterbox web client and the BreakThru HTTP proxy server. He has also completed significant work on several protocol enhancements, including the HTTP binding and end-to-end encryption. Ian is a member of the XMPP Council for 2005-2006, 2006-2007, and 2007-2008.
