XSF People :: Kevin Smith

Kevin Smith is Chair of the XMPP Council for 2008-2009, having previously served as a Council member for 2006-2007 and 2007-2008. Kevin is the project leader on Psi, a popular open-source client for Jabber/XMPP communications. Kevin is also the co-author of several XMPP extensions. Kevin's weblog is located at <http://www.kismith.co.uk/wordpress/>.
