XSF People :: Jack Moffitt

Jack Moffit is CTO at Collecta and a longtime contributor to the open-source community, both technically (Icecast, Ogg Vorbis, Debian) and organizationally (as manager of the Xiph project). He is an expert on the integration of XMPP and web technologies, the topic of his book Professional XMPP Programming with JavaScript and jQuery. Jack is a member of the XSF Board of Directors for 2009-2010; he also previously served on the Board for 2007-2008 and on the XMPP Council for 2005-2006 and 2009-2009.
