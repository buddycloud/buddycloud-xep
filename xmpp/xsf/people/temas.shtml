XSF People :: Thomas Muldowney

Thomas Muldowney (a.k.a. 'temas') joined the Jabber project when it was first announced on Slashdot in January 1999. In addition to working on the jabberd server, Gabber, the JabberStudio developer service, and several other code projects, he was a member of the XMPP Council from 2002 through 2005.
