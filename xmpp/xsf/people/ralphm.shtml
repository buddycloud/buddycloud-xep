XSF People :: Ralph Meijer

Ralph Meijer is the lead developer of several Jabber-related projects and services, including the Idavoll pubsub component, the Mimir notification service, and Planet Jabber. Ralph has been a member of the XMPP Council for 2004-2005, 2005-2006, 2006-2007, 2007-2008, and 2008-2009.
