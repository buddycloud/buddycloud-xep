XSF Contact Page

The XMPP Standards Foundation provides public mailing lists related to Jabber/XMPP programming, server administration, client use, and protocol development. Please post there first, since you will probably receive a faster response!

If your question is not answered in available documentation and is not appropriate for one of the public mailing lists, feel free to contact XSF Executive Director Peter Saint-Andre with any questions or concerns.

You can also contact the XMPP Standards Foundation at the following postal address:

XMPP Standards Foundation
1899 Wynkoop Street, Suite 600
Denver, CO 80202 USA

Financial contributions are accepted (mostly from XSF Sponsors) either by check to the foregoing mailing address or by PayPal to <paypal@xmpp.org>. Because the XMPP Standards Foundation is recognized as a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization, your contribution might be tax deductible.
