Jabber Trademark Licensing Program

Background

Licensing Decision Process

Applications are provided by completing the License Agreement or the online application form.

All decisions regarding approval of licenses for using the Jabber trademark are made by the Trademark Committee of the XMPP Standards Foundation, following the usage guidelines.

In some cases (e.g., for many open-source projects), the Trademark Committee is empowered to waive the licensing fee.
